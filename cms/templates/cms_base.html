About Us

About FOSSEE Workshop Portal

The FOSSEE Workshop Portal is an IIT Bombay initiative that helps institutions host high-quality, practical workshops on free and open-source software. Our goal is to make technical learning accessible, hands-on, and career-relevant for every learner.

We collaborate with mentors, faculty, and campus coordinators to deliver structured learning experiences that combine fundamentals, guided practice, and real implementation.
